# AI Video Prompts FAQ

## What makes a good AI video prompt?
A good AI video prompt is specific, visual, and action-oriented. It usually includes a subject, movement, scene, and visual style.

## Why organize prompts by model?
Different models may respond differently to style, motion, and detail density. Model hubs make prompt discovery easier and improve SEO targeting.

## Why include templates and guides?
Templates help users build their own prompts faster, while guides support broader search intent such as how to write better prompts.

## Can these prompts be tried in HitPaw?
Yes. This repository is designed to guide users from prompt discovery to practical testing in HitPaw Online.
