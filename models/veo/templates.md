# Veo Prompt Templates

## Commercial formula
Product + key feature + background + lighting + camera movement + ad tone

Example:
A premium smartwatch floats above a dark glossy pedestal, blue rim lighting, slow camera orbit, sleek luxury technology advertisement.

## Storytelling formula
Main subject + action sequence + environment + cinematic shot + emotional tone

Example:
A young musician walks alone through an empty city after rain, neon reflections, medium tracking shot, reflective and emotional mood.

## Lifestyle formula
Subject + everyday use scenario + setting + motion + visual style

Example:
A woman uses a tablet at a sunlit cafe table, subtle hand movement, warm soft focus, modern lifestyle ad look.
