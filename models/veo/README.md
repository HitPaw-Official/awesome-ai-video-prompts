## Why use this hub

This hub is meant for users who want practical Veo prompt ideas they can reuse, remix, and adapt for different creative goals.

## Recommended use

For Veo-focused prompts, it is especially helpful to define:

- core subject
- motion and scene progression
- camera perspective
- visual quality and tone
- ad or storytelling intent
