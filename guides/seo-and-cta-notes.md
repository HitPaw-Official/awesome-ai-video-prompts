# SEO and CTA Notes

## SEO strategy

This repository uses a two-layer SEO structure:

1. broad keywords at the top-level library
2. model-specific long-tail keywords in subpages

### Broad keyword targets
- ai video prompts
- text to video prompts
- ai video prompt library

### Model keyword targets
- seedance prompts
- veo prompts
- best seedance prompts
- best veo prompts

## CTA strategy

CTA language should feel practical rather than overly promotional.

Recommended CTA styles:
- Try this prompt in HitPaw AI Video Generator
- Generate this prompt with HitPaw Online
- Explore more AI video models in HitPaw
